TimeStamp Service

Are you interested in knowing what the date is for a unix timestamp or what the unix timestamp is for a natural date?

Well if you are this is the servic for you!

Input your date in either format as shown below and press enter to get your result

Example

https://gbtimestamp.herokuapp.com/api?date=5775885857

https://gbtimestamp.herokuapp.com/api?date=February 7, 2015
